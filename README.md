# YAARA

### A friend you didn't know you needed.

> **Everyone deserves to be heard.**

---

Not everyone has someone to talk to.

Some people hesitate because they don't want to burden their friends.
Some fear being judged.
Some simply need someone who will listen without interrupting.

YAARA was built for those moments.

It is a privacy-first, voice-based AI companion that listens with empathy, understands emotional context, and adapts its conversations to the person—not just their words.

Because everyone deserves to be heard.
